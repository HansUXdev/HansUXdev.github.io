Hardware Prototyping and Product Design

Hans McMurdy February 20, 2016 3 min read
On my spare time and as a hobby, I enjoy working with arduino to design and prototype potential hardware products. I'm not going to lie, I suck at this. But hey its a hobby that is extremely rewarding because at the end of the day you have something tangible. I just wish I was better at it or at least had the time to dedicate to learning how to use CAD, EAGLE and all the various skills required to build a physical product. Here is an example of the prototype smartwatch I was working on back in 2013-2014. At the time, I was really excited for the Apple Watch or iWatch as everyone thought it was going to be called. In particular, I was inspired by all the speculation and concept art that was flying around the internet but thought that apple was going to mess it up and frankly I still think the apple watch has a lot of room for improvement. I started out my drawing some basic sketches, then building out a more detailed prototype with pure html, css and javascript.
